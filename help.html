Help

Need help navigating through our page?

Navigate to the registration option within the navigation bar to register with DDL or log in if you are an existing user. Uncertain about locating a suitable halal restaurant? No issue. Navigate to the Map/Location feature and select your preferred area. Our curated selection of establishments in London awaits your exploration. Once you've chosen a location, simply scroll down to discover venues that cater to your preferences. If you find a particular venue appealing, mark it as a favorite with a single click.

Interested in exploring our new feature? Direct your attention to the navigation bar and select Healthy Eats. Here, you'll have the opportunity to provide a few details about your goal and biometric information, enabling us to recommend suitable dining options. Upon generating your results, select the range at the bottom that aligns with your score to access a curated list of restaurants tailored to your goal.

To manage your events and reservations, access the Calendar/Reminders feature in the navigation bar and input your event details. Curious about 'Your Voice'? This feature allows you to share feedback, suggestions for improvement, concerns, or ratings with us. We value your input, regardless of its nature or scope.

Thank you for chosing Dish Discover London!
